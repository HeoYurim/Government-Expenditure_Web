When Alice follows the White Rabbit hole, she finds herself in a land where she can change her size. She meets a Duchess with a pig for a baby. a Cook that throws spoons, a wise but mad Cheshire cat, the March Hare Mad Hatter and a Dormouse. A Caterpillar shows her how to adjust her height by eating from the right and left sides of a mushroom.
